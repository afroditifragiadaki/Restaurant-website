Discovering the Hidden Gems of Heraklion

Posted on August 5, 2023 by Afroditi Fragkiadaki

 

Heraklion, the capital city of Crete, Greece, is a vibrant and historical destination that offers much more than meets the eye. While many visitors flock to the popular tourist spots, there are hidden gems scattered throughout the city that are waiting to be discovered. In this article, we will take you on a journey to explore some of these hidden gems.

 

1. The Venetian Fortress

Nestled on the hill of Koules, overlooking the picturesque harbor, stands the magnificent Venetian Fortress. Built during the 16th century by the Republic of Venice, this fortress played a crucial role in protecting the city from invasions. Take a stroll along its ancient walls and enjoy panoramic views of Heraklion.

 

2. The Historical Museum of Crete

Located in the heart of Heraklion, the Historical Museum of Crete is a treasure trove of Cretan history and culture. From ancient artifacts to Byzantine art, the museum offers a fascinating insight into the island's rich past. Don't miss the famous painting of "The Baptism of Christ" by El Greco.

 

3. The Old Town District

Step into the charming streets of the Old Town district and get lost in its narrow alleys. Here, you'll find traditional shops, local tavernas, and hidden courtyards. Discover the remnants of the city's Venetian and Ottoman past as you explore the historic buildings and landmarks.

 

4. The Natural History Museum of Crete

For nature enthusiasts, a visit to the Natural History Museum of Crete is a must. Learn about the unique flora and fauna of the island, as well as its geological history. The museum also features interactive exhibits and a life-sized replica of the extinct Cretan dwarf elephant.

 

5. The Farmers' Market

Experience the vibrant atmosphere of the Farmers' Market, where locals gather to sell fresh produce, artisanal products, and traditional delicacies. Taste the flavors of Crete as you sample olives, cheese, honey, and other local treats. Don't forget to haggle for the best prices!

These are just a few examples of the hidden gems that await you in Heraklion. Take the time to explore the lesser-known corners of the city, and you'll be rewarded with a deeper appreciation for its history, culture, and natural beauty.
